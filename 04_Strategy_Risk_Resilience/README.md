## Purpose
This folder contains **executive-level governance artifacts** that connect technical controls to business risk, regulatory expectations, and strategic maturity.

It is designed to answer three questions quickly:
1. **What risks matter most?**
2. **Which controls mitigate those risks?**
3. **How does governance mature over time?**

## Governance Philosophy
- Controls exist to **reduce risk**, not generate documentation.
- Evidence is produced as a **byproduct of operations**, not manual effort.
- Governance is most effective when it aligns identity, security, and finance.
- Maturity is incremental, measurable, and defensible.
